Academic Departments

Explore our diverse range of subjects designed to prepare students for higher education and professional success.

Subject Image Department Course Description
Mathematics Focuses on logical reasoning and problem-solving through Algebra, Geometry, and Trigonometry to build a strong analytical foundation.
Sciences A hands-on exploration of the natural world, covering Biology and Chemistry with a focus on laboratory experiments and scientific inquiry.
Business Introduces students to the world of commerce, including Economics and Accounting, teaching the fundamentals of financial management.
Information Technology Covers digital literacy, document processing (EDPM), and introductory coding to prepare students for the modern tech landscape.
Arts & Literature Enhances communication skills and critical thinking through the study of classic literature, creative writing, and social studies.
