Marching Band

Our international Marching Band is the heart of school spirit. Students learn musical theory, instrument discipline, and coordination, performing at major national events and school rallies.

Dancer

The JKA Dancers celebrate culture and creativity through movement. From traditional folk dances to modern choreography, this group focuses on expression, fitness, and artistic excellence.

Football (Soccer)

Our football program emphasizes sportsmanship and tactical play. The team competes in regional tournaments, fostering a strong sense of community and physical endurance.

Volleyball

The volleyball team is dedicated to agility and strategic teamwork. Through rigorous training and competitive matches, students build leadership skills and lasting friendships on the court.
